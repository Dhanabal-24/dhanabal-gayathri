First Conversation
October 19, 2020. It all began with a simple "H" on Instagram, a message that seemed ordinary at the time. Soon, Instagram chats turned into WhatsApp conversations, and those conversations became a part of our everyday lives.
The Most loved Question
Unexpected Question

Hour of Love
A Silent Proposal
“Kavala padadhinga, Naan iruken,” a line I didn’t fully understand at that time. Later, I came to know that it was actually a hint I didn’t realize then.
Affection became love
A Beautiful "Yes"
After a week of overthinking and with the fear of getting caught by family, Dhanabal finally gathered the courage and said, “Yes, I too love you.” It wasn’t just a reply—it was a feeling he had been holding back for days, finally finding its way out, all because of her patience, her words, and the way she made him feel safe enough to say it.
Soon After
First Meeting
Around 2019–2020, we crossed paths at school as strangers, never knowing our story had already begun.
Late nights
First Call
Our first call wasn't long—just around a minute. Yet, after months of chatting, that brief conversation became a moment we'll always remember.
A special day
First In Person Meeting
After months of conversations, we finally met at her home. What seemed like a simple visit became one of the most memorable moments of our journey.
Captured Together
Our First Image Together
It was around 7:30 PM, a hushed evening when her home stood empty as everyone had gone to town. In that brief window of solitude, we met outside her house, spoke softly, and captured our first selfie together. That exact minute still lingers in my memory, as if time itself paused to preserve it.
Always remembered
Favorite Memory
On the evening of our first meeting, we stood together on her terrace, watching the sunset paint the sky. Between conversations, photos, shared noodles, and quiet moments, an ordinary evening became one of our most cherished memories.
Travel Diaries
First Card Ride
On Pongal, we started our first car ride together from Anjac College and spent some time roaming around the city. Between the conversations, laughter, and little moments along the way, a simple drive became a memory we'll always cherish.
