Our Story

Every chapter matters.

The milestones that brought us here, told one moment at a time.

The Beginning
First Conversation
October 19, 2020. It all began with a simple "H" on Instagram, a message that seemed ordinary at the time. Soon, Instagram chats turned into WhatsApp conversations, and those conversations became a part of our everyday lives.
The Most loved Question
Unexpected Question
One question. One silly reply. A lifetime of memories waiting to begin.
Hour of Love
A Silent Proposal
“Kavala padadhinga, Naan iruken,” a line I didn’t fully understand at that time. Later, I came to know that it was actually a hint I didn’t realize then.
Affection became love
A Beautiful "Yes"
After a week of overthinking and with the fear of getting caught by family, Dhanabal finally gathered the courage and said, “Yes, I too love you.” It wasn’t just a reply—it was a feeling he had been holding back for days, finally finding its way out, all because of her patience, her words, and the way she made him feel safe enough to say it.
Soon After
First Meeting
Around 2019–2020, we crossed paths at school as strangers, never knowing our story had already begun.
Late nights
First Call
Our first call wasn't long—just around a minute. Yet, after months of chatting, that brief conversation became a moment we'll always remember.
A special day
First In Person Meeting
After months of conversations, we finally met at her home. What seemed like a simple visit became one of the most memorable moments of our journey.
Captured Together
Our First Image Together
It was around 7:30 PM, a hushed evening when her home stood empty as everyone had gone to town. In that brief window of solitude, we met outside her house, spoke softly, and captured our first selfie together. That exact minute still lingers in my memory, as if time itself paused to preserve it.
Always remembered
Favorite Memory
On the evening of our first meeting, we stood together on her terrace, watching the sunset paint the sky. Between conversations, photos, shared noodles, and quiet moments, an ordinary evening became one of our most cherished memories.
Travel Diaries
First Card Ride
On Pongal, we started our first car ride together from Anjac College and spent some time roaming around the city. Between the conversations, laughter, and little moments along the way, a simple drive became a memory we'll always cherish.

Travel
Our Little Ride
15th April 2024
The day our first-floor housewarming was done, I came to pick you up before your mom arrived. We went on a short bike ride for barely fifteen minutes, but somehow it became one of those moments that stayed with me. No grand plans, no destination—just us, the open road, and a memory I'll always cherish.
Special
Birthday Celebration
24th June 2021
A surprise that took eight months of planning and eight months to bring to life. The moment it finally happened made every day of waiting worth it.
Outdoors
Chennai Outing
18th July 2025
A metro ride across Chennai, lunch together, shopping at D-Mart, exploring Nexus Mall, seeking blessings at Vadapalani Temple, visiting the Kalaignar and Jayalalithaa Memorials, and ending the day by the waves of Marina Beach—every stop had its own charm, but the best part was sharing it all with you.
Chats
Endless Conversations
Ongoing
We spent the whole day talking, teasing, arguing, laughing, and somehow never running out of things to say. Looking back, those little moments became the best part of the day.
Memorable Train
The coach next door
20th September 2024
When she boarded the Mysuru Express for her college IV trip to Bangalore, he boarded too—choosing an unreserved compartment just for the chance to see her. He traveled with her until Erode, proving that sometimes the shortest moments create the longest-lasting memories.
Homecoming
When home met "Her"
29th November 2023
With my parents away for a family function, I finally got the chance to show her my home. From room to room, she explored every corner while we spent some quiet time together. A simple drive there and back, but a memory that made home feel a little more special.
